Time Tables

The Survey is divided to two courses, both of which are on Fridays.

Do not consider this list final - if you have good working knowledge on an important subject that is not covered in the tracks but wish to lecture on it, make an offer - changes can be made. However, they are much more likely to accepted for the short lectures, since the major lectures must cover the most important technologies influencing each track.

Short Subjects

For both courses, these are the deadlines for the short subjects:

May 25th: Email a ZIP file with all HTML files, graphic files, source code and anything else your online presentation of the subject includes.

Projects

For both courses, these are the deadlines for the practical projects:

June 8th: Submit a project description, including which technologies will be used, what the application will look like and do, which tools will be used to build it, and an initial design (should be 1-3 pages long).

July 29th: Five weeks after end of semester: final submission.

For both the short subjects and the final project, people who give the main lecture on the submission date or one week before or after it, can request a postponement of the deadline.
