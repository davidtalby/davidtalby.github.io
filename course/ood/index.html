July 1st, 2006
The final exam will take place on Monday, July 10th at 14:00 in Canada Hall (check with the faculty for last minute changes). The resources page includes past exams with solutions, and this year's exam will be in the same format. The material for the exam includes all lectures and exercises. Material that appeared in the theoretical exercises or guest lecture is included as well, although questions about it will generally be at a lower level of detail.
There won't be a formal review meeting before the exam, but I plan to be at Ross 108 in the morning before the exam, so feel free to come and ask whatever questions you have.
Good luck!!
